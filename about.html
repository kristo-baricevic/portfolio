My name is Kristo Baricevic. I am a fullstack developer from Philadelphia, PA.

As a software developer, I believe in working hard and finding solutions. I am a people-first developer, meaning I want my clients and my team to feel seen and heard. I believe in getting the whole team past the finish line and feeling good about themselves and the product.


Outside of coding, I am a musician and an artist. In fact, I started a record label and production company called Big Muddy Records upon graduating high school. That journey took me all the way up to 2018!


One of the things that sets me apart as a software developer is my resilience and my patience. I have learned that you have to work very hard to get the results you want, and this often comes with mistakes and failures. Errors are a clue about a path forward, and a missed bullseye is a chance to shoot again.


I am growth-oriented, which means I am always challenging myself to grow in new ways. I've also recently run my first half-marathon, and if you had told me that a year ago I would not have believed you!
