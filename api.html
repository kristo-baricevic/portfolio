Find Similar Artists

One of the best parts about developing is playing with APIs. There's so many tools out there for us to play with, and this is just one example. So many people struggle to find new music to listen to, so I wanted to demonstrate how easy it could be to find other artists that sound like your current favorites.

Did you know that you can use Spotify's recommendations algorithm in any app or website? Just enter any artist's name and get 5 similar artists.

Then, if you are feeling adventurous, you can click on the artist's name to perform a Google search!





Your results will populate here! Thanks for playing :)
